üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Voice Recognition Problems

  • Ensure microphone permissions are granted
  • Speak clearly and at normal volume
  • Record for at least 3-5 seconds
  • Check internet connection

Zoom Integration Issues

  • Verify Zoom account permissions
  • Re-authorize if connection fails
  • Check if Zoom app is updated
  • Ensure stable internet connection

Calendar Sync Problems

  • Re-authorize Google Calendar access
  • Check calendar permissions
  • Verify time zone settings
  • Ensure calendar is not private

Mobile App Issues

  • Update to latest app version
  • Clear app cache/data
  • Restart the app
  • Check device storage space
