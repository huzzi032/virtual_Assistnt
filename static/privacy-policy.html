Virtual Assistant - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Virtual Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our voice assistant application and Zoom integration services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Voice Data

  • Audio recordings for speech-to-text processing
  • Transcribed text from your voice commands
  • Meeting recordings and transcriptions (with your consent)

2.2 Zoom Integration Data

  • Zoom account information (name, email)
  • Meeting metadata (meeting IDs, titles, participants)
  • OAuth authorization tokens

2.3 Calendar Data

  • Calendar events and scheduling information
  • Meeting details and participant information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process voice commands and provide assistant responses
  • Schedule and manage calendar events
  • Integrate with Zoom for meeting management
  • Extract and organize todos and action items
  • Send email notifications and reminders

4. Data Processing and Storage

  • Voice data is processed using Azure OpenAI services
  • Data is stored securely on Microsoft Azure infrastructure
  • Voice recordings are processed temporarily and not permanently stored
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted and stored securely

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Microsoft Azure (for AI processing services)
  • Google (for calendar integration)
  • Zoom (for meeting integration)

6. Data Retention

  • Voice recordings: Processed immediately and not stored
  • Transcriptions: Stored for 30 days for service improvement
  • OAuth tokens: Stored until revoked by user
  • Calendar data: Synchronized with your calendar provider

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Revoke authorization for integrations
  • Export your data

8. Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable data protection laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional privacy regulations.
